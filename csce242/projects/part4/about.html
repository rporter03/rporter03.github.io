About

You might be asking yourself right now... "what are niche sounds?" But the question you need to know is, what IS Niche Sounds? Well, I'm here to answer that! Niche Sounds is a cool website where I, as well as other users such as yourself, share cool, unknown songs and artists for anybody to discover! My purpose is simply that; to provide a space where people can share and discover amongst themselves. Niche Sounds is also a place of musical discussion; yap about all of your interests here!
